Trail is launching soon!

The Constitution Trail connects people from many diverse backgrounds. From students to trail enthusiasts, the trail provides a way for the community to interact. We seek to streamline this process by providing an intuitive way for our users to get to know the trail. With Trail, you can pinpoint important areas on the trail. You can find water fountains, set custom meeting points, you can even visualize and plan routes! In the future Trail will even be able to track your own personal statistics about how you interact with the trail! Give it a try, and let us know what you think!
